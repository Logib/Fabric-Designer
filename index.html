OUR STORY

Thread Fashions began with a simple idea to provide high-quality education and resources to aspiring fabric designers. Our founders, seasoned professionals in the textile industry, recognized the need for accessible, comprehensive training that covers both traditional techniques and modern innovations. Today, we are proud to offer a range of master classes that cater to designers at every stage of their journey.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every fabric tells a story and every designer has the tools to bring their visions to life. By fostering a community of creative minds, we aim to inspire and support designers to push the boundaries of textil e design. Our courses are designed not just to teach, but to ignite a passion for fabric design that leads to lifelong learning and innovation.
